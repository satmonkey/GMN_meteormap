Dimensions used:

v_init - Initial speed (trajectory start point)
v_g - Geocentric velocity (m/s)
v_h - Helioventric velocity of the meteor (m/s)
rend_ele - End height
peak_magn - Peak magnitude
duration - Visible trajectory duration (s)
ra_g, dec_g - Geocentric coordinates
L_g, B_g - Geocentric ecliptic coordinates
L_h, B_h - Heliocentric ecliptic coordinates
SCE_g - Geocentric ecliptic coordinates relative to solar longitude
SCE_h - Heliocentric ecliptic coordinates relative to solar longitude
a - Semi-major axis (AU)
e - Eccentricity
i - Inclination
peri - Argument of perihelion
pi - Longitude of perihelion
q - Perihelion distance (AU)
QQ - Aphelion distance (AU)
Tj - Tisserand's parameter with respect to Jupiter
